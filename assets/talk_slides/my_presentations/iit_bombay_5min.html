Research Profile

  1. Probabilistic broadcast with coded packets
    • Proposed and analysed a probabilistic forwarding mechanism with coded packets.
    • Counterintuitive result: Using additional coded packets can reduce energy consumption.

  2. Community detection on geometric graphs
    • Conditions when no algorithm can recover the communities.
    • Linear-time local algorithm for community recovery.

  3. Community detection on hypergraphs
    • Proposed a semi-definite algorithm for recovering communities using the similarity matrix.

  4. Spatial queues
    • Analysed nearest neighbour dynamics in a queueing setting.
    • Characterized the fraction of overloaded servers for queues that are spatially distributed.
